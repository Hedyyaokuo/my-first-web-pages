  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Blueberry tart

To make a simple blueberry tart, you'll need a pie crust, fresh blueberries, sugar, cornstarch, and a bit of lemon juice. Roll out the pie crust, place it in a tart pan, mix blueberries with sugar, cornstarch, and lemon juice, then fill the crust with the mixture. Bake until the crust is golden and the filling is bubbly.
